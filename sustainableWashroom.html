Sustainable Toilet Use Tips

Tips on Water Conservation

  • Fix any leaks in your toilet or plumbing system to prevent wasting water.
  • Use a low-flow or dual-flush toilet to reduce water usage.
  • Don't use your toilet as a trash can to avoid unnecessary flushes.
  • Consider using a bidet or installing a bidet attachment to reduce toilet paper usage.

Tips on Energy Conservation

  • Use natural lighting or energy-efficient light bulbs in your bathroom.
  • Turn off the lights and exhaust fan when leaving the bathroom to save energy.
  • Use a low-flow showerhead to reduce water usage and the energy required to heat the water.

Tips on Reducing Waste

  • Use recycled or biodegradable toilet paper to reduce waste.
  • Use a composting toilet to turn waste into fertilizer for plants.
  • Avoid flushing items such as cotton balls, Q-tips, and feminine hygiene products that can clog pipes and damage sewage systems.
